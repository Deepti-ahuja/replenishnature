Save Biodiversity




The variety of life on earth is commonly referred to as biodiversity. It plays an important role in functioning of food chains and webs. It also helps to maintain ecological balance of ecosystem. International day for Biological diversity is observed every year on May 22. The main causes of biodiversity loss are habitat loss, over-exploitation, introduced species, ecosystem disruption.

A biosphere reserve is an international conservation designation given by the UNESCO under its programme on 'Man and the Biosphere(MAB)' The IUCN maintains an international list of animals and plants whose continued existence is getting threatened published in the form of Red Data Book. Awareness about in situ and ex situ conservation should be spread. Now lets consider the most important species on earth which is bees(YES!). It is believed that if bees get extinct, existence of human beings is impossible. Because of cross pollination we get 70% of crops of food. Bees pollinate vegetables like broccoli, onion, egg plant ; fruits like kiwi, mango, apple ; coffee; cotton and much more.

Environmental Issues

Acid Rain



Compounds like sulfur dioxide and nitrogen oxides emitted mainly by factories come in contact with the water vapours present in atmosphere causing acid rain. Acid rain can cause marble cancer, wash nutrients from soil, has adverse effect on aquatic life as well.

Ozone depletion



CFCs cause depletion of ozone layer present in stratosphere by decomposing ozone to oxygen, causing ozone hole through which UV rays can pass. Start using CFC free refrigerator, it's URGENT. UV rays can cause serious health issues. Stop making ozone hole larger.
